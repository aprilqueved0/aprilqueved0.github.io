April Quevedo is a data reporter based in Washington, D.C.

She's covered infrastructure, business, state elections and the Latino community in Maryland. Her work has appeared in the Baltimore Sun, the Baltimore Banner and the Capital Gazette. Quevedo is a graduate student at the Philip Merrill College of Journalism and a fellow at the Howard Center for Investigative Journalism. Prior to working in journalism, she spent nearly a decade in the logistics and supply chain management industry.

Quevedo is a proud Chicago native and lover of all things Abraham Lincoln.
